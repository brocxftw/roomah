## Why

The current Campaigns page is a basic list that does not support the campaign command-centre experience defined in `resources/campaigns-design.json`. Users need to monitor operational campaign performance, compare spend and conversion efficiency, open external campaign links, and act on campaigns without leaving the workspace.

## What Changes

- Replace `/app/campaigns` with a ROOMAH master-detail workspace aligned with the Leads and Properties modules: operational KPI cards, single-row filters, campaign performance table, and a persistent right-side insight drawer.
- Use channel bucket only for campaign classification. No new `campaign_type` data model is introduced in this change.
- Add an optional external campaign URL to campaigns so users can paste the link generated by Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, Threads, Google, or another platform and open it from campaign rows and drawer actions.
- Show a six-card operational KPI row: Active Campaigns, Total Spend, Leads Generated, Conversions, Average Cost per Lead, and Conversion Rate.
- Add filter controls for campaign search, status, channel, date range, and reset. Keep filters concise and operational rather than report-heavy.
- Replace the table rows with a performance-oriented campaign grid that surfaces campaign identity, channel, status, period, spend, leads, conversions, cost per lead, and external-link/action affordances.
- Add deep-linked drawer selection via `/app/campaigns?campaign=<id>&tab=<tab>`.
- Add a campaign insight drawer with Overview, Performance, Leads, and Timeline tabs. The drawer shows campaign context, performance summaries, attributed leads, synthetic timeline history, and quick actions without requiring page navigation.
- Keep analytics concise: use KPI cards and operational summaries, not large charts or external-platform reporting.
- Add drawer quick actions for Edit Campaign, View Leads, Pause Campaign, Duplicate Campaign, and Open External Link when a campaign URL exists.
- Reuse the existing campaign creation page for editing and duplication by supporting `?edit=<campaign_id>` and `?duplicate=<campaign_id>` query parameters.
- Add campaign filtering to the Leads workspace/API so "View Leads" can open the Leads workspace scoped to the selected campaign.
- Keep campaign content templates out of this change; they are proposed separately in `add-campaign-content-templates`.

## Impact

- **Frontend**: Updates `frontend/src/app/app/campaigns/page.tsx` and `frontend/src/app/app/campaigns/new/page.tsx`. May add reusable campaign workspace components for KPI cards, filters, table rows, drawer tabs, and campaign action controls.
- **Frontend routing**: Adds `/app/campaigns?campaign=<id>&tab=<tab>` selection behavior and campaign wizard query modes for `edit` and `duplicate`.
- **Backend**: Updates campaign create/update models and routes to accept `external_url`; updates lead listing filters to support a `campaign_id`/`campaign` filter used by the drawer "View Leads" action.
- **Data model**: Adds nullable `external_url` to `marketing_campaigns`. No `campaign_type` column is introduced.
- **Out of scope**: Campaign content templates, external platform publishing, external platform analytics ingestion, persisted campaign timeline events, and large campaign reporting dashboards.
